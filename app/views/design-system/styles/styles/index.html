Styles

Digital services for government staff users in the Home Office (new services and existing digital services that are being redeveloped) should use these styles.

In the Home Office this includes:

  • caseworking systems
  • microsites
  • intranets

The styles are available in a Home Office version of the prototype kit.

Why we created these styles

If your service is not on a gov.uk or service.gov.uk domain, you cannot use the GOV.UK crown logo (Service Manual, Services for government users) or the GDS Transport font.

Most Home Office services for government staff users fall within these restrictions. For those services, these styles replace the GOV.UK styling.
