Advanced Services

Your pharmacy must be offering Medicine Use Reviews (MUR) or New Medicine Service (NMS), or must be registered for the NHS Urgent Medicine Supply Advanced Service (CPCS) Pilot.

NHSmail

Pharmacy staff at the pharmacy must be able to send and receive NHSmail from their shared NHSmail account, which must have at least two live linked accounts.
